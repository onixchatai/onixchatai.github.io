Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can my chatbot be set up?

Most businesses are live within 48-72 hours. We handle all the technical setup, menu integration, and staff training. You just need to provide your menu and business information.

Will customers actually use a chatbot?

Yes! Our clients see 70-80% of customer inquiries handled by the chatbot. Customers love the instant responses and 24/7 availability, especially for quick questions about hours, menu items, and placing orders.

What if I need changes to my chatbot?

We include unlimited menu updates and basic customizations. For major changes, we work with you to implement them quickly. Our goal is to ensure your chatbot perfectly represents your business.

Does this work with my existing POS system?

Yes, we integrate with most major POS systems including Square, Toast, Clover, and others. Orders flow directly into your existing workflow without any extra steps for your staff.

What kind of support do you provide?

All plans include setup, training, and ongoing support. Professional and Enterprise plans get priority phone support. We're local to Burbank, so we understand the unique needs of Valley businesses.
